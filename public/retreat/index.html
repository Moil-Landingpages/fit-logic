Questions

Everything you need to know

Escape & Exhale is for women 21+ experiencing burnout, chronic stress, or disconnection — especially high-functioning professionals who are managing everything on the outside but running on empty inside. No prior retreat or wellness experience needed.

Research in stress physiology shows a minimum of 3–4 days is needed for meaningful nervous system downregulation. Weekend retreats allow only surface-level relaxation. Four full days allows cortisol levels to measurably decrease and deeper integration to occur.

This is not a fitness retreat. All movement is gentle, restorative, and optional — somatic movement, nature walks, breathwork. Everything supports recovery, not physical performance. If you want to rest instead of a session, that is exactly what you should do.

Escape & Exhale is a wellness and education retreat, not a medical treatment program. Megan's clinical background informs the science-based programming, but this is not a substitute for medical care.

Potentially. Self-employed individuals and business owners may deduct wellness and professional development expenses. We recommend consulting your accountant — we'll provide an itemized receipt.

Cancellation policy to be confirmed. Full details will be provided with registration.

This is an intentionally small-group retreat. The small size ensures real personal attention from Megan and genuine community rather than a crowd.

Comfortable clothes for movement and rest, layers for Hill Country evenings, a journal, and an open mind. A full packing list is sent upon registration. Leave the laptop at home — you'll be glad you did.
